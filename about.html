About Us

New Dhiman Timber Store is a Professional Quality Timber Store & Hardware Components Store in Rajpura, Punjab, India. Since 1983, we have been your premier destination for high-quality timber and hardware components. Explore our wide range of products, including wooden door frames, wooden doors, main door, mosquito net wire door, wooden windows, furniture, flush door, wooden bed, wooden almirah, center table, dressing table, flush door, wooden cupboard, wooden office cabinet, wooden desk, wooden chairs and more. New Dhiman Timber Store also offers furniture repair services and provides assistance with any furniture-related needs by offering in-home visits.
